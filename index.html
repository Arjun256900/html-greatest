Greatest of all time

1. Ronaldo

Cristiano Ronaldo is a world-renowned Portuguese professional footballer who is widely considered one of the greatest football players of all time. He was born on February 5, 1985, in Funchal, Madeira, Portugal. Ronaldo is known for his incredible speed, agility, scoring ability, and versatility on the field. Throughout his illustrious career, Ronaldo has played for several top clubs, including Sporting CP, Manchester United, Real Madrid, Juventus, and he returned to Manchester United in 2021. He has won numerous awards and accolades, including multiple FIFA Ballon d'Or awards, which are given to the world's best player. Ronaldo has an impressive goal-scoring record, breaking numerous records and achieving remarkable milestones. He is known for his incredible work ethic and dedication to the sport. Off the field, he is also involved in various philanthropic efforts and is considered a global sports icon. Ronaldo's impact on the world of football is immense, and he continues to be a highly influential and successful player in the sport.

2. Kylian Mbappe

Kylian Mbappé is a highly talented French professional footballer who has rapidly risen to prominence as one of the brightest stars in the world of soccer. He was born on December 20, 1998, in Bondy, France. Known for his incredible speed, dribbling skills, and clinical finishing, Mbappé has been compared to some of the game's legends. Mbappé began his professional career with AS Monaco and quickly gained attention for his exceptional performances. In 2017, he transferred to Paris Saint-Germain (PSG), where he has continued to shine and establish himself as one of the best players in the world. Kylian Mbappé has won numerous awards and titles, including domestic league titles with PSG. He played a crucial role in France's victory at the 2018 FIFA World Cup, where he was awarded the FIFA World Cup Best Young Player. Mbappé is considered one of the most exciting prospects in football and is often praised for his humility and sportsmanship off the field. His future in the sport looks incredibly promising, and he remains a key figure in the world of football.

3. Neymar

Neymar, whose full name is Neymar da Silva Santos Júnior, is a Brazilian professional footballer known for his exceptional skills, creativity, and flair on the field. He was born on February 5, 1992, in Mogi das Cruzes, São Paulo, Brazil. Neymar is widely recognized for his dribbling abilities, precise ball control, and goal-scoring prowess. Neymar's professional career began with Santos FC in Brazil, and he quickly gained international attention with his performances. In 2013, he made a high-profile move to Barcelona, where he formed a formidable attacking trio with Lionel Messi and Luis Suárez. During his time at Barcelona, he won numerous titles, including the UEFA Champions League. In 2017, Neymar transferred to Paris Saint-Germain (PSG) for a world-record fee, becoming one of the most expensive footballers in history. He has since continued to excel with PSG, winning domestic titles and contributing significantly to the team's success. Neymar is also a vital part of the Brazilian national team and has represented his country in multiple FIFA World Cups and Copa America tournaments. Off the field, Neymar is known for his philanthropic efforts and is a global icon in the world of sports. His talent and personality have made him one of the most recognizable and marketable athletes worldwide.

4. Vinicius

Vinícius Júnior, often referred to simply as Vinícius Jr., is a talented Brazilian professional footballer who has gained recognition for his electrifying style of play and promising potential. He was born on July 12, 2000, in São Gonçalo, Brazil. Vinícius Jr. is known for his exceptional speed, dribbling skills, and ability to take on defenders with flair. Vinícius Jr. started his professional career with Flamengo in Brazil, and his performances there quickly caught the attention of scouts and football enthusiasts. In 2018, he made a high-profile transfer to Real Madrid, one of the world's most renowned clubs. His time at Real Madrid has seen him continue to develop as a young talent with great promise. While still relatively early in his career, Vinícius Jr. has shown glimpses of his immense potential, contributing to Real Madrid's success and being seen as one of the rising stars of world football. He has also represented the Brazilian national team, showcasing his skills on the international stage. His dynamic and entertaining style of play has made him an exciting prospect for the future of football.
